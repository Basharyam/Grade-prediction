About GradePredictor

A student‑first tool that uses machine learning to forecast grades and help you plan smarter.

Overview

GradePredictor uses data‑driven methods to estimate your outcome in a selected subject. Enter your background and recent scores, and we’ll provide a fast, interpretable prediction.

Key Goals

  • • High accuracy for future course predictions.
  • • Fast decisions with instant results.
  • • Scalable to many students and long histories.
  • • Big‑data ready ingestion and inference.
  • • Low latency (<120ms typical).

The Model

We currently use K‑Nearest Neighbors (KNN). The model finds similar learners (“neighbors”) and infers your likely result based on their outcomes. With proper encoding and scaling, KNN offers simple, reliable behavior and improves as data grows.

Technology

  • ML: Python (KNN, preprocessing)
